Bioidentical Hormone Replacement

Hormone Replacement Therapy Chattanooga

BHRT is a natural hormone — not made from synthetic solutions. It is used for both men and women to alleviate the symptoms of pre-menopausal conditions (cramps, PMS, endometriosis, irregular periods, hot flashes, mood swings), post-menopausal symptoms (vaginal thinning & dryness, reduced libido, mood swings), andropause (low testosterone, fatigue, reduced libido, mood swings), as well as anxiety and depression.

  • Natural, not synthetic — derived to replicate the body's own hormonal levels
  • Available for both men and women
  • Addresses pre-menopausal, post-menopausal and andropause symptoms
  • Helps with anxiety, depression, fatigue and mood swings
  • Multiple delivery methods: pellets and troches available
  • Sustained hormone levels without roller coaster fluctuations

Hormone Pellet Therapy for Men & Women

Pellet therapy uses hormones derived to replicate the body's normal hormonal levels. Implants placed under the skin consistently release small, physiologic doses of hormones that provide optimal therapy. Unlike oral and transdermal forms — which produce "roller coaster" hormone levels causing mood and energy fluctuations — the BioTE® Medical Method using bio-identical hormone pellet therapy is the only delivery method that provides sustained hormone levels throughout the day for up to three to five months without any roller coaster effect.

Hormone Troches for Men & Women

Troches are small lozenges that dissolve between the cheek and gum over about 30 minutes. As it dissolves, hormones are gradually absorbed into the bloodstream — resulting in physiologically natural hormone blood levels. Because these are bio-identical to the hormones your body has produced, they are efficiently recognized, utilized, metabolized and excreted by the body.
