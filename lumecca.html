Lumecca IPL Treatment in Chattanooga, TN

One of the most obvious signs of healthy and youthful skin is an even skin tone. Patients become concerned about hypo- and hyperpigmentation as they age. Lumecca and other energy-based treatments are the most effective way to restore even skin tone.

Lumecca is a highly effective intense pulsed light (IPL) treatment for pigmented and vascular lesions. You will notice a significant improvement in the complexion and clarity of your skin after just one use. Lumecca uses photothermolysis to provide a comfortable and effective light treatment.

Benefits of Lumecca *Individual results may vary

  • Removes age spots
  • Spider veins and other vascular lesions are reduced
  • Rosacea treatment
  • Reduced freckles
  • Sun damage is repaired

Significant Improvement After Just One Session

After treatment, redness and a slight warming of the skin are normal and usually go away within an hour. Pigmented lesions will darken slightly then clear in the weeks following — revealing a more even, luminous skin tone. Having multiple sessions yields even better results.

What to Expect After Treatment

Your Recovery & Results

After the first session, the skin's appearance has improved. Having multiple sessions yields even better results. Individual outcomes may differ, and contraindications may apply. An experienced, licensed professional must perform this treatment.

Consultation with an experienced, licensed professional will help you determine whether Lumecca treatment is an effective and safe treatment option for you.

Pigmented Lesions

You may notice a darkening of pigmented spots over the next 1–2 days. The pigmented lesions will clear in the weeks following, resulting in an even skin tone.

Vascular Lesions

Following treatment for vascular lesions, you may notice the vein blanching or a color change in the vessel, which will fade after a few days.

Redness & Warmth

After treatment, redness and a slight warming of the skin are normal and usually go away within an hour of your appointment.

Multiple Sessions

After the first session, the skin's appearance is already improved. Having multiple sessions yields even better, longer-lasting results.
