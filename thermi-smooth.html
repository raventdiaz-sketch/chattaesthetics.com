ThermiSmooth Face & Body

ThermiSmooth® is a cutting-edge non-invasive skin tightening treatment available right here in Chattanooga, Tennessee at Medical Aesthetics of Chattanooga. The Science Behind ThermiSmooth

How ThermiSmooth Works

ThermiSmooth uses a thermistor-regulated handpiece that gently heats the skin's surface using controlled radiofrequency energy. Unlike treatments that target only the surface, ThermiSmooth delivers heat to the deeper layers of the dermis where collagen lives — triggering the body's natural collagen remodeling response for lasting tightening results.

Radiofrequency Heat

Controlled RF energy is delivered precisely to the skin's surface and deeper dermal layers, warming tissue to the optimal temperature for collagen stimulation without discomfort.

Collagen Remodeling

The controlled heat triggers your body's natural collagen production and remodeling process — gradually tightening and smoothing skin over time from the inside out.

Face & Body

ThermiSmooth is uniquely versatile — treating delicate facial areas like crow's feet as well as larger body areas like the stomach, making it one of the most comprehensive tightening options available in Chattanooga.

Zero Downtime

ThermiSmooth treatments are comfortable and require absolutely no recovery time. Chattanooga patients can return to all normal activities immediately after each session.

Where Can ThermiSmooth Be Used?

ThermiSmooth can be used around the eyes to reduce crow's feet, around the mouth to smooth lip lines, along the cheeks for lifting, on the neck for tightening loose skin, and on the body including the stomach for skin tightening — all without any surgery or recovery time required for Chattanooga patients.

ThermiSmooth FAQs — Chattanooga

Who is a good candidate for ThermiSmooth in Chattanooga?

ThermiSmooth is ideal for patients who want to tighten loose or crepey skin on the face or body without surgery. It works well for both men and women of all skin types. During your free Chattanooga consultation, our providers will evaluate your skin and recommend the right treatment plan for your goals.

How many ThermiSmooth treatments will I need?

Most Chattanooga patients see the best results with a series of treatments — typically 3 to 6 sessions spaced a few weeks apart. As our 10-month before and after results show, consistent treatment delivers remarkable cumulative improvement over time.

Is ThermiSmooth painful?

ThermiSmooth is widely described as comfortable and even relaxing by our Chattanooga patients. The handpiece delivers a warming sensation to the skin — similar to a warm massage — with no pain or discomfort.

Can ThermiSmooth treat both face and body areas?

Yes! ThermiSmooth is one of the most versatile tightening treatments available. It effectively treats delicate facial areas including crow's feet, lip lines, cheeks and neck — as well as larger body areas like the stomach, inner arms and thighs.
