Obagi Skintrinsiq™ in Chattanooga

At Medical Aesthetics of Chattanooga, we offer Obagi Skintrinsiq™ treatments for skin rejuvenation. Obagi Skintrinsiq™ also reduces unwanted blemishes and uneven skin texture.

To tackle a variety of skin issues, the Skintrinsiq device employs cutting-edge technology including blue and red LED light therapy, as well as the highest-grade components found in Obagi's skincare products. This innovative skincare gadget has four treatments — all of which stimulate thorough skin cleansing and collagen stimulation for a gorgeous appearance with no downtime.

Four Treatments, Zero Downtime

The Skintrinsiq device combines blue and red LED light therapy with Obagi's highest-grade skincare formulations — delivering collagen stimulation, deep cleansing, and skin renewal in four specialized facial treatments, all with no downtime required.

The Four Skintrinsiq™ Facials

01

Antioxidant Facial

For those who have photodamage, this antioxidant facial is ideal. It uses Obagi's Professional-C serum to infuse your skin with L-ascorbic acid — the only form of Vitamin C that can be applied topically. This serum penetrates deep into your skin, providing antioxidant protection from UV damage while brightening hyperpigmentation and encouraging collagen and elastin fiber formation. Hyaluronic acid prevents dryness by binding moisture to your skin.

Fine LinesWrinkles Dry SkinSensitive Skin Sun DamageAge Spots Acne Scars
02

Brightening Facial

From cell to surface, the brightening facial helps you achieve healthier, renewed, and revitalized skin. The Obagi-C RX serum — containing a combination of 10% Vitamin C and 4% hydroquinone — is used to stimulate skin renewal from the inside out. This facial is designed for individuals who seek a more even skin tone and a reduction in fine lines and wrinkles.

HyperpigmentationMelasma Sun DamageAge Spots Dry or Oily SkinUneven Texture
03

Clarifying Facial

Designed for customers who have oily skin prone to breakouts. The Obagi Clenziderm M.D. pore therapy used in this facial promotes skin clarity by keeping pores clear and regulating excess oil production. This facial will help you achieve a more balanced, clear, and revitalized complexion.

Oily SkinBreakouts Clogged PoresExcess Oil
04

Firming Facial

For clients with fine lines, wrinkles, and a desire for firmer-looking skin. This firming facial employs Obagi's Elastiderm Facial Serum — made with a unique Bi-Mineral Contour Complex utilizing zinc, copper, and malonate — designed to enhance elastin protein formation to retain the skin's bounce and elasticity. Ideal for those with aging skin concerns.
