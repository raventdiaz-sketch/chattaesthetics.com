The Process

How CoolSculpting Works

A targeted cooling process called Cryolipolysis combats fat cells underneath the skin, freezing and reducing them — while leaving healthy skin cells unharmed.

01

Targeted Cooling

A non-invasive applicator is placed on the treatment area. Controlled cooling is delivered precisely to the subcutaneous fat layer beneath the skin.

02

Fat Cell Death

Fat cells are more vulnerable to cold than surrounding tissues. The cooled cells undergo apoptosis — natural controlled cell death — while skin, nerves and muscles remain unaffected.

03

Z-Wave Technology

Immediately after treatment, we apply Z-Wave technology — improving your results by up to 60% compared to manual massage by evenly distributing fat cells for a smooth, even outcome.

04

Natural Expulsion

Over the following weeks and months, your body naturally processes and eliminates the dead fat cells through its normal metabolic processes.

05

Visible Results

Most patients see a noticeable fat reduction within 2–4 months. Additional sessions can enhance results further if desired.

06

Long-Term

As long as you maintain your normal diet and exercise, your CoolSculpting results should remain stable long-term — fat cells don't come back once eliminated.

Your Questions Answered

CoolSculpting FAQ's

Everything you need to know about CoolSculpting — how it works, what to expect, and why it's right for you.

CoolSculpting is a non-invasive procedure that effectively reduces fat bulges in targeted areas using advanced cooling technology. There are no needles, no incisions, no anesthesia, and minimal recovery time. It is performed in a medical office under physician supervision.

A non-invasive applicator delivers precisely controlled cooling to the treatment area. As heat is extracted, the subcutaneous fat cools and fat cell death is triggered through a process called Cryolipolysis. The result is a visible fat reduction within two to four months in most patients.

Cryolipolysis is based on the principle that fat cells are more vulnerable to energy extraction (cooling) than surrounding tissues. The cooled fat cells undergo apoptosis (controlled cell death) and are gradually expelled through the body's normal metabolic processes, reducing the fat layer over time.

Unlike lasers, radiofrequency, or focused ultrasound, CoolSculpting's controlled cooling targets only fat cells without affecting surrounding tissue. It does not burn, shatter, or extract non-fat cells, so there is no downtime needed for your body to heal.

Yes. ZELTIQ™ has performed controlled clinical studies showing that CoolSculpting is an effective way to reduce fat bulges in properly selected patients. Since becoming commercially available, these results have been repeated with millions of patients worldwide.

Lipids in fat cells crystallize at warmer temperatures than other water-containing cells. This is how Cryolipolysis selectively targets only fat cells for destruction while sparing skin, muscle, and nerves. The CoolSculpting system precisely controls the rate of energy extraction to ensure safety.

Unlike many procedures, CoolSculpting requires minimal to no downtime. After your treatment you can typically return to your normal daily activities right away. Each treatment lasts about one hour — easily fit into a lunch break.

After one visit you'll typically see a noticeable fat reduction. It takes a few months to fully realize the effects, as it takes that long for your body to naturally dispose of the fat cells. You can see even more improvement with additional appointments if your doctor deems it appropriate.

Yes. Fat cells that are frozen during CoolSculpting gradually die off and leave the body through its natural expulsion process — which is why the results can be long-term. As long as you maintain your normal diet and exercise, your results should remain stable. Untreated areas will have no change in fat cell distribution.
