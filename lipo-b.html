Lipo-B Injections in Chattanooga

Lipo-B is a multi-ingredient injection containing essential B vitamins, minerals and amino acids. It provides nutrients to your body which effectively promotes increased metabolism that will in-turn directly assist your body in burning fat. Many of the nutrients in the Lipo-B injection are not easily absorbed through the mouth and are therefore highly effective when received via injection.

The amino acids play a major role in minimizing excess fat in the liver, increasing energy and lean muscle mass, neutralizing free radicals, as well as metabolizing fat and cholesterol to prevent hardening of the arteries.

Superior Absorption via Injection

Many of the nutrients in the Lipo-B injection are not easily absorbed through the mouth — making the injectable form far more effective at delivering the metabolic benefits your body needs to burn fat and boost energy.

Key Benefits

What Lipo-B Does for You

Boosts Metabolism

Promotes increased metabolism that directly assists your body in burning fat more efficiently than diet alone.

Increases Energy

Essential B vitamins delivered directly into the bloodstream for immediate energy enhancement and mental alertness.

Liver Support

Amino acids minimize excess fat in the liver and help metabolize fat and cholesterol to prevent hardening of the arteries.

Lean Muscle Mass

Supports the development and maintenance of lean muscle mass while neutralizing harmful free radicals in the body.
