The Science Behind the Lift

How Ultherapy Works

Ultherapy uses micro-focused ultrasound energy to reach the deep structural layers of skin that are typically only addressed in surgery — without disrupting the surface of the skin. This energy triggers the body's natural regenerative response, stimulating the production of fresh collagen that lifts and tightens skin over time.

Ultrasound Energy

Focused ultrasound energy is delivered precisely to the foundational layer of skin — the same layer addressed in surgical facelifts — without any incisions.

Collagen Stimulation

The ultrasound energy triggers your body's natural healing response, stimulating the production of new collagen that gradually lifts and firms the skin.

Progressive Results

Results develop naturally over 2 to 3 months as fresh collagen forms. Most Chattanooga patients see continued improvement for up to 6 months after treatment.

No Downtime

Unlike surgical procedures, Ultherapy requires no recovery time. Our Chattanooga patients return to their normal daily activities immediately after treatment.

Natural, Progressive Lifting Results

Ultherapy stimulates your own collagen production deep beneath the skin's surface. Results appear gradually over 2–3 months as fresh collagen develops — producing a natural, progressive lift that looks and feels completely authentic because it IS your own collagen.

Common Questions

Ultherapy FAQs — Chattanooga

Who is a good candidate for Ultherapy in Chattanooga?

Ultherapy works best for patients with mild to moderate skin laxity who want a non-surgical lift. Ideal candidates are typically in their 30s to 60s with skin that has begun to loosen but still has some elasticity. During your free Chattanooga consultation, our providers will evaluate your skin and determine if Ultherapy is right for you.

How long does an Ultherapy treatment take?

Most Ultherapy treatments at our Chattanooga location take between 30 and 90 minutes depending on the areas being treated. The face and neck together typically take about 60 to 90 minutes, while a smaller area like the brow may take just 30 minutes.

When will I see Ultherapy results?

Some patients notice an immediate effect, but the real results develop gradually over 2 to 3 months as new collagen is produced. Many Chattanooga patients continue to see improvement for up to 6 months after their treatment as the collagen building process continues.

Is there downtime after Ultherapy?

No. Ultherapy requires virtually no downtime. Some patients experience mild redness or tingling immediately after treatment, but most return to normal activities the same day. This makes it a popular choice for busy Chattanooga patients.

How do I get started with Ultherapy in Chattanooga?
