What is Xeomin?

Xeomin, produced by Merz Aesthetics, is an FDA-approved botulinum toxin A that acts as a therapeutic muscle-relaxing agent. Available right here in Chattanooga, Tennessee, Xeomin received FDA approval in 2011 for the temporary improvement of moderate to severe frown lines between the eyebrows in adults under 65.

Known as a "naked" neuromodulator, Xeomin is uniquely free from complexing proteins — meaning it carries a reduced risk of antibody resistance over time compared to other neuromodulators. This makes it an excellent long-term option for Chattanooga patients who want consistent, natural-looking results.

  • FDA approved since 2011 for frown lines between the brows
  • Results typically last 3 to 4 months initially
  • After several treatments, results may last 4 to 8 months or longer
  • Free from complexing proteins — reduced antibody resistance risk
  • No downtime — return to daily activities immediately
  • Results fade gradually and naturally
  • Administered by experienced, credentialed providers in Chattanooga

Why Xeomin?

Xeomin vs. Other Neuromodulators

Chattanooga patients often ask how Xeomin compares to Botox or Dysport. While all three are botulinum toxin A products, Xeomin stands apart because it contains no accessory proteins. This "naked" formulation means your body is less likely to develop resistance over time — keeping your treatments effective for the long haul.

Pure Formula

Xeomin contains only the active botulinum toxin A with no additives or complexing proteins, reducing the chance of antibody resistance with repeated treatments.

No Refrigeration Required

Unlike some neuromodulators, Xeomin does not require refrigeration before reconstitution, ensuring consistent potency and easier storage for your provider.

Natural Results

Our Chattanooga providers use precise injection techniques to deliver subtle, natural-looking results — smoothing lines without a frozen appearance.

Quick Treatment

A Xeomin session typically takes 15–20 minutes with no downtime. Many of our Chattanooga patients come in on a lunch break and return to work the same day.

What Results Will I See?

The effects of Xeomin usually begin appearing within 3–7 days and fully develop over 2 weeks. Results typically last 3 to 4 months initially. Following several treatments, results may last 4 to 8 months or longer. Individual results may vary.

Common Questions

Xeomin FAQs — Chattanooga

How is Xeomin different from Botox?

Both are botulinum toxin A products, but Xeomin is a "naked" neuromodulator — it contains no accessory proteins. This may reduce the chance of developing antibody resistance over time, making it a great long-term choice for patients in Chattanooga who receive regular treatments.

How long does Xeomin last in Chattanooga patients?

Most patients see results lasting 3 to 4 months with initial treatments. Over time, with consistent treatments, many patients experience results lasting 4 to 8 months. Your provider will discuss a personalized treatment schedule during your free consultation.

Is there downtime after Xeomin injections?

No. Xeomin requires virtually no downtime. Most patients return to normal activities immediately. We recommend avoiding strenuous exercise and lying flat for 4 hours after treatment.
