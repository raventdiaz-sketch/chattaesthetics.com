M'lis Body Contour Wrap Chattanooga

Developed by a bio-chemist from UCLA Medical Center, this wrap was originally formulated to encourage circulatory and lymphatic flow in patients. It was meant for those suffering from diabetes, or any disease that inhibited circulation, especially in the lower extremities.

Research showed that due to the increase in the flow of both the lymphatic and the circulatory systems, the patient was also experiencing cleansing of the tissues, which in turn, causes healthy inch loss through internal cleansing. Not only does the body benefit internally, but the external benefits of contouring and inch loss made this wrap ideal for spa industries and the medical setting.

Inch loss, diminishing of cellulite and body contouring is completely safe. Many body wraps have come and gone but this one has been around for over 25 years.

Internal & External Benefits

The M'lis wrap works from the inside out — cleansing tissues through improved lymphatic and circulatory flow while delivering visible inch loss and cellulite reduction on the outside. Warm, luxurious, and completely safe.
