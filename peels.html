Revolutionary Chemical Peel

The Perfect Peel

The Perfect Peel is a revolutionary chemical peel that contains the ingredients to transform your skin into looking younger, healthier and clearer in just one week. The Perfect Peel can be used on all skin types and requires no pre-peel skin preparation.

What makes The Perfect Peel so unique is that it is founded on a key anti-aging ingredient never before used in a chemical peel — called Glutathione. Treating all skin types safely, this peel improves skin texture, brightens, reduces lines and wrinkles, lightens pigmentation, evens out skin tone, and treats conditions such as congestion and acne.

The Perfect Peel is designed to be used every 3 to 6 months depending on your skincare regimen, and results continue to improve with each peel. It is fast acting, easy to apply, and virtually painless — with only 5–7 days of peeling required.

  • Improves the overall tone and texture of your skin
  • Lightens and brightens your skin
  • Removes or reduces sun damage and age spots
  • Reduces pore size and softens fine lines and wrinkles
  • Reduces or eliminates hyperpigmentation
  • Stimulates collagen production resulting in firmer skin
  • Suitable for all skin types
