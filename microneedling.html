Microneedling in Chattanooga, TN

Microneedling (SkinPen) treatments in Chattanooga, TN is perhaps the most mainstream skin health management treatment performed. Microneedling helps decrease wrinkles and scars without warmth or removal. The SkinPen utilizes a cycle known as automated micro-needling which makes a number of small pokes directly in the skin to instigate collagen.

Collagen Induction Therapy (CIT) utilizes the body's common reaction to recuperating to expand the collagen and elastin in the skin. Collagen and elastin are normally found in the skin, and they help give the skin a solid sparkle and a more energetic appearance.
