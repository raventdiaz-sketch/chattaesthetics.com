Hyaluronic Acid Filler

Júvederm® Chattanooga

The effects of time and aging on your facial skin can be less than desirable — wrinkles and the loss of volume result in deepening lines and folds. Juvéderm® helps combat obvious signs of aging by recovering youthful fullness and smoothing away wrinkles and folds.

Juvéderm® is made of hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance in your skin that helps add volume and hydration. Your own hyaluronic acid may diminish over time as you age, contributing to changes in your face — particularly wrinkles and folds.

Talk to one of our injection specialists about the advantages of choosing Juvéderm® for your laugh lines, smile lines, nasolabial folds ("parentheses" on either side of the nose/mouth), and marionette lines (lines running downward from either side of your mouth).

*Individual Results May Vary

Are You a Candidate for Juvéderm®?

If you notice wrinkles, loss of facial volume, deepening lines or folds — you may be an excellent candidate. Talk to one of our injection specialists about the advantages of choosing Juvéderm® as your cosmetic filler solution.

Natural Cosmetic Dermal Filler

Restylane® Fillers

Restylane® restores volume and fullness to the skin to diminish moderate to severe facial wrinkles and folds, such as nasolabial folds. It is a safe and natural cosmetic dermal filler also used to plump lips and define the edges of lips.

Restylane® has a stabilizing technology that helps maintain the cosmetic effect until the injected material is nearly gone. Studies have shown that Restylane® injections generally last about six months. Treatments are typically scheduled twice a year, and having a touch-up treatment before the product has fully dissipated may enhance the lasting effect.

*Individual Results May Vary

HA Injectable Filler

Belotero Balance® Dermal Filler

As we age, the skin loses its youthful appearance as natural support structures — such as hyaluronic acid (HA) — diminish over time, resulting in wrinkles. BELOTERO BALANCE® Dermal Filler is an injectable filler that quickly integrates into your skin, filling in facial wrinkles and smoothing them out instantly.

While BELOTERO BALANCE® is made of the same HA material as some other dermal fillers, its unique properties allow it to adapt within the skin for soft and even correction. The versatility of the product makes it strong enough for deep treatment areas such as nasolabial folds, as well as more delicate areas such as vertical lip lines — thin lines that appear above the lip line.

*Individual Results May Vary
