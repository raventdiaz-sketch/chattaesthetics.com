About April

Chattanooga's Most Trusted Injector

With almost three decades of experience in healthcare, April Johnson brings a refined, results-driven approach to aesthetic medicine that is simply unmatched in Chattanooga. After earning her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga in 1998, she spent more than 20 years specializing in Wound Care — developing a keen eye for detail and a strong foundation in healing and skin that most injectors never come close to achieving.

April is trained and certified through the American Academy of Facial Esthetics (AAFE), combining clinical excellence with an artistic approach to deliver safe, natural, confidence-boosting results. She is committed to helping patients feel refreshed and empowered while maintaining a focus on safety and individualized care.

She doesn't just treat faces — she studies them. Every contour, every feature, every unique characteristic is considered with precision and artistry before a single touch is made. Her patients don't just look better — they feel like the most powerful version of themselves.
