Kybella in Chattanooga

If a double chin has been the bane of your existence — and the reason for a lack of a good selfie photo — there is now a reason to rejoice and keep your chin up, literally. That reason is Kybella, which is FDA-approved as a nonsurgical injectable treatment to dissolve fat under the chin.

Fat Can Be Melted Away

The Science

How Kybella Works

Immediately after the fat dissolving injection is released, it causes lysis — destroying the fat cell membrane. As a result, the cells break down and release their fatty contents. The body recognizes the fat dissolving injection substance in a similar way the liver recognizes bile, and naturally metabolizes it before the by-product is excreted.

Non-Surgical

FDA-approved injectable treatment — no knives, no incisions, no surgery required. Performed right in our office.

Fat Cell Lysis

The injection destroys fat cell membranes on contact, causing cells to break down and release their fatty contents naturally.

Natural Excretion

The body metabolizes and naturally excretes the destroyed fat — similar to how the liver processes bile.
