Full Body Treatment

Morpheus8 Body

Morpheus8 Body is the first and only FDA-approved treatment for deep 8mm subdermal adipose tissue remodeling. The one-of-a-kind large surface tip is made up of 40 thin gold-coated microneedles with a thermal profile of up to 8mm.

Morpheus8 Body can rapidly treat large tissue surface areas using the most powerful RF energy available. This technology uses clinically proven Morpheus8 technology to remodel subdermal and dermal tissue through fractional coagulation of fat and bulk sub-necrotic heating — providing a quick and effective solution for treating larger body areas for fat reduction, skin remodeling, and resurfacing.

Equipped with Burst mode RF Vulcanization technology, Morpheus8 Body automatically deploys bipolar RF energy to multi-level treatment depths in a single cycle. The ability to target tissue sequentially at three levels in millisecond intervals reduces treatment times significantly, minimizes skin injury, increases treatment uniformity, and enables customized full body fractional procedures.

  • Targets tissues sequentially at three levels for maximum effectiveness
  • Significant time savings compared to other body treatments
  • Safe for all skin types with little risk of post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation
  • 40 thin gold-coated needles reaching up to 8mm subdermal tissue penetration (7mm + 1mm thermal profile)
  • Burst technology delivers bipolar RF energy at multiple depths in a single cycle
  • Improves treatment consistency across large surface areas
