InMode EmpowerRF

A New Standard in
Women's Wellness

At Medical Aesthetics of Chattanooga, we proudly introduce EmpowerRF by InMode — an exceptional multi-functional platform designed to cater to women's wellness needs. This innovative in-office system offers a comprehensive range of therapies to address various concerns including strengthening weak pelvic floor muscles, relieving stress, urge, and mixed urinary incontinence, managing pain, enhancing blood circulation, and fortifying abdominal muscles.

EmpowerRF helps restore patient self-confidence and quality of life without the need for surgery or extended downtime. Our synergistic technology handpieces enable tailored, layered procedures that effectively target a wide range of issues with swift, safe, and effective delivery.

The EmpowerRF System

Our Technology Handpieces

FDA Cleared

VTone

VTone is a gentle, FDA cleared technology designed to provide intravaginal electrical muscle stimulation (EMS) and neuromuscular re-education to rehabilitate weak pelvic floor muscles, and address stress, urge, and mixed urinary incontinence. A comfortable, non-invasive treatment that delivers meaningful results for women's intimate wellness.

Deep Tissue Heating

FormaV

FormaV is a comfortable device that provides uniform volumetric deep heating for tissue treatment with real-time temperature control. This precise thermal delivery promotes tissue remodeling and improves overall tissue quality, restoring comfort and confidence with no downtime required.

Minimally Invasive

Morpheus8 & Morpheus8V

Morpheus8 and Morpheus8V are minimally invasive treatments designed for fractional coagulation of subcutaneous tissue. Morpheus8 provides an interchangeable portfolio of microneedling tips to deliver customizable fractional treatments — targeting deep layers of skin and tissue for comprehensive rejuvenation and tightening.

Hands-Free EMS

Tone

Tone works by emitting pulses of electrical muscle stimulation (EMS) to simulate involuntary muscle contractions on body areas. Tone's hands-free applicators target specific muscle groups to restore the strength of the muscle — an effective, comfortable treatment for abdominal toning and core strengthening without surgery or downtime.
